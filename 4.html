Meet Dr.Yinka Femi
Yinka Femi is a beloved software developer with speciality in variety of computer languages ranging from javascript, laravel, python. He is also an enterpreneur, digital marketer and lifestyle businessman. He overcame career adversity at an early age. despite his success in business, femi's greatest joys are spending time with his family and friends.

At fofx (ef-of-ex), we believe in the power of learning. Given the rapidly changing nature of our world and dazing speed of advances in technology, we believe that the most important skill we can teach you is the ability to learn. We believe that learning who, when, where and how to ask the right questions is more important than knowing the right answer, because in today’s digital world, the answer is always changing.

fofx’s mission is to help people learn how to learn, unlearn and relearn the skills they require to have a successful career in an era of digital transformation.

A common feedback we had been hearing from clients was difficulty finding tech talent to hire. This was not due to a shortage of applicants but the inadequate skill demonstrated by the applicants in the selection process, even though their resumes read like an honour roll. We believe this can be addressed by equipping our students not only with the theoretical knowledge, but practical skills and the ability to update those skills by themselves as the technical know-how demanded by the labour market evolves.
